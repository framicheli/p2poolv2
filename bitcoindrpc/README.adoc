== BitcoindRPC

This crate is used by p2poolv2 to make bitcoind API calls.

We only support the endpoints that we need to call. As we need more,
we will add more here.

List of supported calls:

Chain & mempool:

. getbestblockhash
. getblock
. getblockchaininfo
. getblockcount
. getblockfilter
. getblockhash
. getblockheader
. getmempoolentry
. getnetworkinfo
. getrawmempool
. getrawtransaction
. gettxout

Fees / broadcast / validation:

. estimatesmartfee
. sendrawtransaction
. testmempoolaccept

Decode:

. decoderawtransaction
. decoderawtransaction_hex (proxy-friendly hex-string version)

Internal use:

. getdifficulty
. getblocktemplate
. submitblock
